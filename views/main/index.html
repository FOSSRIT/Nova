A round glass building on a campus of brick cubes, the Center for Student Innovation is a mixing bowl ;-) for collaborative innovation that thinks outside the discipline.

This website is a Virtual Innovation Center for our community, where People and Groups generate and develop Ideas, Projects and Events. RIT innovators watch these pages for innovation opportunities (projects, collaborators, ideas, opportunities to make a contribution, etc.). Don't put any proprietary information up here by accident.

The best way to have a great idea is to have a lot of ideas. And the best way to have a lot of ideas is by sharing them, and combining them with others.

Our Informational Website is at http://innovation.rit.edu.
